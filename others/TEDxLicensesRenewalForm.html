What amazing work is being done in your community -- research, discoveries, insights, theories, solutions, etc. -- which is largely unrecognized?

Include variety! Your event must explore different subject areas, not just one. For example, “Social entrepreneurship” or “Sustainability” might seem broad but talks on different aspects of social entrepreneurship or sustainability still result in an event focused around only one subject.

Your Co-organizer
Will you be co-organizing your event with someone else? If so, enter their name here as it appears on their TED.com profile.

Your Event Location
Even before deciding on your event’s venue, you should know roughly where you'd like your event to take place. At a minimum, you will need to fill out the “City” and “Country” fields. We use this information to place your event on the map of global TEDx events. If your license is approved, you can update your event page with more precise information.
